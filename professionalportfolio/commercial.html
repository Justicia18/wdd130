Commercial Projects

These commercial projects were designed throughout my time at BYU-I and show case my growth and learning.

Archer Rd School House Redesign

School House to Restaurant

For this project I redesigned an old school house on Archer road near Rexburg, ID. The redesign involved updating the exterior finishes to not only fix it up but to also give it a more modern feel. The added railing and porch accomadates an outdoor eating area to enjoy summer weather. All while keeping this building a symbol of the areas long history.

Mixed-Use Building

56 W 2nd N, Rexburg, ID

This mixed-use buiding was designed to accomadate three stores on the first floor, a lobby, managment office, and mail area. The apartments in the upper floors offer studio and 1-bedroom apartments. The building was designed to encourage business growth in the area and accomadate the growing population in the growing college town.
