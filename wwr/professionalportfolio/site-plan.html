Home page

Tyler Justesen, an esteemed professional in the construction industry, has embarked on a remarkable journey from his formative years in construction to becoming a seasoned expert in Virtual Design and Construction (VDC).

Hailing originally from the lively coastal city of Virginia Beach, Virginia, Tyler's upbringing was steeped in the culture of construction, instilling in him a strong work ethic and a knack for hands-on problem-solving. His pursuit of a career in construction led him to Brigham Young University - Idaho, where he immersed himself in the world of Virtual Design and Construction.

During his tenure at Brigham Young University - Idaho, Tyler dedicated five years to his education in VDC, honing his skills and knowledge in the field. Bolstered by three years of practical experience, he emerged as a proficient and innovative practitioner, at the forefront of leveraging technology to revolutionize construction processes.

Beyond his professional accomplishments, Tyler is a devoted husband, celebrating 5.5 years of marriage, and a proud father to a lively 2-year-old son. The warmth of family life extends to their cherished 3-year-old dog. While Tyler's roots may lie in Virginia Beach, he has found a welcoming home in the tranquil surroundings of Rexburg, Idaho.

In addition to his professional and family life, Tyler is a well-rounded individual with a diverse set of interests. Whether exploring the rugged landscapes of Idaho with his family, staying abreast of industry trends through continuous learning, or seeking solace in the great outdoors, Tyler brings a wealth of experiences to every aspect of his life.

Tyler Justesen's narrative is one of dedication, growth, and a steadfast commitment to excellence. From his upbringing amidst construction sites to his pivotal role in VDC, he continues to leave an indelible mark on the construction industry while finding fulfillment in the balance of professional achievement and the joys of family life.

Residential

Welcome to my portfolio of residential architecture projects, where every structure tells a unique story of innovation, inspiration, and the art of living. Within these pages, I invite you to embark on a journey through a collection of homes that epitomize the essence of thoughtful design, meticulous craftsmanship, and the pursuit of creating spaces that resonate with the human spirit.

In this portfolio, you will discover an array of residential projects that span a spectrum of styles, contexts, and aspirations. From charming bungalows nestled in quaint neighborhoods to contemporary masterpieces gracing urban landscapes, each project reflects my unwavering commitment to excellence, creativity, and the seamless integration of form and function.

As an architect and designer, I believe that the homes I create are more than just physical structures; they are reflections of the individuals and families who inhabit them. My approach to residential architecture is rooted in a deep understanding of my clients' needs, aspirations, and lifestyles, allowing me to tailor each design to their unique preferences and personalities.

Throughout this portfolio, you will encounter a diverse array of architectural styles, from modernist interpretations to traditional homages, all unified by a common thread of timeless elegance and meticulous attention to detail. Whether it's optimizing space for maximum efficiency, harnessing natural light to create inviting interiors, or seamlessly integrating indoor and outdoor living areas, my designs are driven by a relentless pursuit of beauty, functionality, and sustainability.

Beyond aesthetics, my commitment to excellence extends to every aspect of the architectural process, from initial conceptualization to final construction. I collaborate closely with my clients, builders, and craftsmen to ensure that each project is executed with precision, integrity, and a steadfast dedication to exceeding expectations.

As you peruse these pages, I hope that you will be inspired by the creativity, innovation, and passion that define my residential architecture projects. Whether you are embarking on your own journey of homeownership, seeking inspiration for your dream home, or simply appreciating the artistry of architectural design, I invite you to immerse yourself in the beauty and possibility of residential architecture. Welcome to my portfolio, where dreams take shape and spaces come to life.

Commercial

Welcome to my portfolio of commercial architecture projects, where innovation, functionality, and the power of design converge to shape the landscapes of commerce and industry. Within these pages, I invite you to explore a diverse collection of projects that exemplify the dynamic intersection of architecture, business, and community.

As an architect specializing in commercial spaces, I am committed to creating environments that not only inspire and engage but also enhance the daily experiences of those who inhabit them. From bustling urban centers to serene corporate campuses, each project in this portfolio represents a unique opportunity to redefine the way we work, shop, dine, and interact with the world around us.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of collaborating with a wide range of clients, from visionary entrepreneurs to established corporations, each with their own distinct vision and objectives. By understanding their needs, aspirations, and brand identities, I strive to design spaces that not only meet their functional requirements but also embody their values and ethos.

In this portfolio, you will encounter a diverse array of commercial projects, ranging from sleek office buildings and retail spaces to vibrant mixed-use developments and innovative community centers. Whether it's creating flexible work environments that foster collaboration and creativity, designing retail spaces that captivate and inspire, or crafting hospitality venues that offer memorable experiences, my designs are guided by a commitment to excellence, sustainability, and the human experience.

Beyond aesthetics, my approach to commercial architecture is grounded in a deep understanding of the economic, social, and environmental factors that shape the built environment. From leveraging cutting-edge technologies to implementing sustainable design principles, I am dedicated to creating spaces that not only stand the test of time but also contribute to the well-being of their occupants and the broader community.

As you explore these pages, I invite you to immerse yourself in the world of commercial architecture and envision the possibilities that lie ahead. Whether you are a business owner seeking to transform your space, a developer embarking on a new project, or simply a lover of architecture and design, I hope that this portfolio serves as a source of inspiration and a testament to the transformative power of architecture in shaping the way we live, work, and connect with the world around us. Welcome to my portfolio of commercial architecture projects, where innovation meets impact and the future takes shape.
